How SmartAgric works ?

Whether you are a smallholder farmer, a cooperative lead, or a buyer, the platform is designed to be simple and practical.

1) Create your farm profile

Add your location, crops, and season. This unlocks localized weather, pests, and market insights tailored to your farm.

2) Explore crop guides

Search the knowledge base for crop-specific best practices, from land prep to post-harvest handling.

3) Check live prices

Visit the market page to compare daily prices, build a watchlist, and get reminders for your target price.

4) Ask an agronomist

Open the advisory chat to share field photos and receive guidance from certified extension officers.

5) Source inputs

Browse vetted suppliers for seeds, fertilizer, and irrigation tools, with verified reviews and delivery estimates.

6) Sell and deliver

Match with nearby buyers and book logistics partners who understand post-harvest requirements.
